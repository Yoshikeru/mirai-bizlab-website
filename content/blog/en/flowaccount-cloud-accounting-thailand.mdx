## What is FlowAccount

**FlowAccount** is a cloud accounting tool used widely, especially among SMEs and small businesses in Thailand. It handles quotations, invoices and receipts, cash-in/out tracking, VAT and withholding tax (WHT) reports, and e-Tax Invoice support — all with a **simple, easy-to-grasp interface**. It also works well from a mobile app, which makes it manageable even at small sites without a dedicated accountant.

## FlowAccount's strengths

- **Easy to adopt, intuitive UI** — even staff with limited accounting background can keep up daily entry
- **Quote-to-cash in one place** — order-related documents and money flow managed together
- **e-Tax Invoice & e-Receipt ready** — aligned with Thailand's e-invoicing scheme
- **Add-ons for adjacent needs** — payroll and more can be added as required

## FlowAccount or PEAK?

Both FlowAccount and [PEAK](/en/blog/peak-cloud-accounting-thailand) are Thai cloud accounting tools. It is not about which is better — the right answer depends on your **scale, operating setup, and HQ reporting requirements**.

- **FlowAccount tends to fit**: early-stage or small sites, and cases where you first want to get off Excel and streamline billing
- **PEAK tends to fit**: detailed department- or outlet-level management accounting, or close co-operation with an accounting firm

The key is not to start from the tool, but to **choose neutrally based on operational requirements and cost**.
